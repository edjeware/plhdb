Database access

To access the Primate Life History Database hosted in the Department of Primate Behavior and Evolution (PrimEvo) at the Max Planck Institute for Evolutionary Anthropology (MPI-EVA), users will need:

  • a guest contract with MPI-EVA
  • an appropriate VPN client
  • Adminer/PostgreSQL credentials
